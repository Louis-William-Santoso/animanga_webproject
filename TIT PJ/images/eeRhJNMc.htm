Re:Produce Your Singing Voice
VOCALOID:AI Voice Bank

  • Real-time Processing

    VOCALO CHANGER PLUGIN operates as a VST3/AU plugin effect, converting audio data into an AI Voice Bank voice in real time. You can alter any of the parameters in DAW automation to perform vocal direction at will.

  • Analysis and Reconstruction

    Unlike conventional voice changers that process singing voices, VOCALO CHANGER PLUGIN analyzes singing voices using AI, and sings them back in a VOCALOID voice to reconstruct them.

  • AI Voice Bank

    VOCALO CHANGER PLUGIN is compatible with VOCALOID:AI Voice Bank. Choose your favorite from a comprehensive lineup of unique Japanese and English male and female singing voices.
